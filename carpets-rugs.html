Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What are the benefits of using Carpet Rugs in home interiors? +
Carpet Rugs add warmth, style, and comfort to your interiors. They reduce noise, provide insulation, and enhance the visual appeal of your space with textures and patterns that suit every décor style.
2. Are Shyamik Overseas products handmade or machine-made? +
We specialize in both handmade and machine-made Carpet Rugs. Our skilled artisans use traditional weaving methods as well as modern technology to ensure premium quality and durability.
3. Can I get customized Decorative Carpet designs? +
Yes! We offer tailor-made solutions for customers who want custom sizes, colors, or designs. Whether it's a single rug or a bulk order, our Carpet Manufacture team ensures perfection in every detail.
4. How do I maintain and clean my Carpet Rugs? +
Regular vacuuming and gentle cleaning help maintain your Carpet Rugs. For stains, use mild detergent and lukewarm water, or get them professionally cleaned to retain their original texture and color.
5. Do you export rugs internationally? +
Yes, Shyamik Overseas exports high-quality Carpet Rugs and Decorative Carpet designs to multiple countries, including the USA, Canada, and across Europe. We are a trusted Rugs Manufacture brand with a strong global presence.
6. What materials do you use for your carpets? +
We use a variety of premium materials including wool, cotton, jute, silk blends, and recycled fibers. Each material is carefully selected for durability, comfort, and visual appeal to meet diverse customer preferences.
