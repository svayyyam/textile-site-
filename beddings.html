India's Premier Beddings Manufacturer

Shyamik Overseas is a leading manufacturer and exporter of premium beddings in India. We specialize in producing high-quality bed sheets, duvet covers, pillowcases, and complete bedding sets that bring ultimate comfort and elegance to every bedroom.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What materials are used in your bedding collections? +
We use a variety of premium materials, primarily focusing on high-quality cotton (including high thread count options), linen blends, and eco-friendly fabrics that ensure breathability and supreme comfort.
2. Do you supply beddings to hotels and the hospitality sector? +
Yes, we are a major supplier to the hospitality industry. We offer durable, hotel-quality beddings that can withstand frequent commercial laundering while maintaining a luxurious feel.
3. Can I order customized bedding sizes and designs? +
Absolutely. We provide comprehensive customization services, allowing B2B clients to specify dimensions, fabrics, thread counts, colors, and unique patterns for bulk orders.
4. How should the beddings be cared for? +
Most of our cotton and linen beddings are machine washable. We recommend washing in cool or warm water with a mild detergent and tumble drying on low to preserve the fabric's integrity and softness.
