FAQs

Q1. What makes handmade cushion covers different from machine-made ones? +
Handmade cushion covers are crafted by skilled artisans using techniques like embroidery, macrame, crochet, tufting, and hand-weaving. Each piece is unique, durable, and carries a personal touch that machine-made products cannot replicate.
Q2. Are cushion throws suitable for both indoor and outdoor use? +
Yes, our cushion throws are designed for both indoor comfort and outdoor styling. We use durable fabrics and finishes that resist fading and wear, making them versatile for different environments.
Q3. Can I order custom-made cushion covers in bulk? +
Absolutely. Shyamik Overseas specializes in custom orders for retailers, wholesalers, online sellers, and hospitality projects. We offer flexibility in size, design, color, and material to meet your business needs.
Q4. How do I clean and care for handmade cushion covers and throws? +
Most handmade cushion covers and throws can be gently hand-washed or machine-washed on a delicate cycle, depending on the fabric. Always avoid harsh detergents or bleach. Dry them in shade to preserve colors and textures.
