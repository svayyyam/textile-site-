Frequently Asked Questions

What products does Shyamik Overseas offer? +
We offer a comprehensive range of home textiles and décor items including Carpets & Rugs, Cushions & Throws, Poufs & Ottomans, and Accessories such as baskets, wall hangings, and planters. All our products are handcrafted with premium materials.
Do you offer custom designs? +
Yes! We specialize in custom designs. Whether you need specific colors, patterns, sizes, or materials, our design team works closely with you to create products that perfectly match your requirements.
What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)? +
Our MOQ varies by product category. We cater to both small boutique orders and large bulk purchases for retailers and wholesalers. Contact us for specific MOQ details for your product of interest.
Which countries do you export to? +
We export to over 53 countries worldwide, with our primary markets being in Europe, the USA, and Canada. We're continuously expanding to new markets including Dubai, Australia, and South Africa.
What materials do you use? +
We use a wide range of premium materials including wool, cotton, jute, silk blends, recycled fibers, and eco-friendly alternatives. All materials are carefully sourced for durability, sustainability, and aesthetic appeal.
Do you have quality certifications? +
Yes, we hold multiple international quality certifications including OEKO-TEX, Sedex, ISO 9001, ICAFIN, GoodWeave, and GOTS. These certifications reflect our commitment to quality, ethical practices, and sustainability.
How can I request a product catalogue? +
You can request our catalogue by clicking the "Request Catalogue" button on any product page, or by contacting us directly via email at info@shyamikoverseas.com or calling +91-0 9992717900.
What is the typical lead time for orders? +
Lead times depend on the product type, quantity, and customization requirements. Standard orders are typically fulfilled within 4-6 weeks, while custom orders may take 6-8 weeks. We'll provide exact timelines when you place your order.
Do you provide samples before bulk orders? +
Yes, we provide samples so you can evaluate the quality, material, and design before committing to a bulk order. Sample charges and shipping costs apply and are typically adjusted against the final order.
How do I get in touch with your team? +
You can reach us through our Contact Us page, email us at info@shyamikoverseas.com, or call us at +91-0 9992717900. Our team typically responds within 24 hours.
