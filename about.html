The Story of Shyamik Overseas

Transparency & Artisan Mastery

A Heritage Built on Vertical Integrity

Based in the historic textile hub of Panipat, Haryana (India), Shyamik Overseas was founded on the belief that world-class home textiles should be built on a foundation of transparency and artisan mastery.

We are a premier, vertically integrated manufacturer and exporter of high-quality home textiles, specialized in bridging the gap between superior craftsmanship and the global retail market by maintaining complete control over simultaneous processes.

As a direct manufacturer, we take pride in our 100% in-house production model. Every product that bears the Shyamik Overseas name is designed, sourced, and manufactured within our own facilities, ensuring that we never compromise on quality, safety, or ethical standards.

Preserving the Human Touch

The Art of the Hand-Skilled Maker

At Shyamik Overseas, we believe that the soul of a product lies in the hands that create it. While Panipat is known for industrial strength, we have dedicated ourselves to preserving the human touch that defines premium home textiles.

01

Precision in Every Knot

Our rugs are brought to life through meticulous hand-tufting and weaving techniques, ensuring a level of texture and durability that mass automation alone cannot replicate.

02

Detail in the Stitch

From delicate embroidery on cushions to refined finishes on bedding, our artisans bring decades of generational knowledge and cultural legacy to every single piece.

03

Vertical Integrity

Because production is 100% in-house, we provide a safe, dignified, and master-led environment for our craftsmen to thrive, keeping the heritage alive.

Empowering Communities

Supporting Women's Craftsmanship
