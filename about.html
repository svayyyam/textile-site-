Our Story

A Legacy of Weaving

A Heritage Woven in Time

Founded over two decades ago, Shyamik Overseas began as a small weaving workshop in the heart of India. Today, we are proud to be one of the premier exporters of handcrafted and machine-made rugs globally.

Our mission is simple: To bring the finest Indian artisanship into homes around the world, elevating everyday living spaces while providing sustainable livelihoods to thousands of skilled weavers. We believe that every rug tells a story—a narrative of tradition, passion, and meticulous attention to detail.

Our Values

Driven by Passion & Integrity

As we have grown to serve over 50 countries, we have never lost sight of our core values. We continuously innovate our designs while respecting the age-old techniques inherited from generations of master craftsmen.

Integrity, quality, and community support remain the cornerstones of our operations. We ensure ethical practices at every stage of production, creating an environment where art and ethics coexist beautifully.

Our Philosophy

The Art of Mindful Craftsmanship

"True luxury lies in the hands of the artisan and the soul of the materials."

We believe that a rug is more than a decorative piece; it is the foundation of a room's character. Our design philosophy intertwines contemporary aesthetics with profound respect for traditional techniques. Every thread woven is a testament to our commitment to creating enduring elegance that transforms spaces into sanctuaries of comfort and style.
