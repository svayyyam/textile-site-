GLOBAL STANDARDS

Pioneering Ethical Manufacturing

We believe that the beauty of a rug lies not just in its design, but in the integrity of its creation. Our certifications validate our dedication to ethical practices, safety, and sustainable sourcing.

OEKO-TEX® Standard 100

Issued by Testex | Since 2012

Ensures that every component of our carpets, from yarns to dyes, has been rigorously tested for harmful substances, making our products safe for human health and the environment.

GoodWeave Certified

Issued by GoodWeave Int. | Since 2010

A commitment to ending child labor in global supply chains. Purchasing a GoodWeave certified rug supports educational opportunities for children in weaving communities.

ISO 9001:2015

Issued by BSI | Since 2008

An international standard dedicated to Quality Management Systems (QMS), assuring our clients that we consistently provide products meeting customer and regulatory requirements.

Sedex SMETA

Issued by Sedex | Since 2015

Empowers our responsible supply chain activities by auditing labor standards, health & safety, environmental compliance, and business ethics.

Global Organic Textile Standard

Issued by Control Union | Since 2018

The worldwide leading textile processing standard for organic fibres, ensuring ecological and social criteria are met from harvesting to manufacturing.

ICAFIN Membership

Issued by ICAFIN | Since 2005

Active participation in elevating the Indian carpet industry's global standing through shared knowledge, export excellence, and best practices.
